About us

Hello! This website is about the instruments included in a concert band. While there are many more instruments included in a marching band or a military band (which also uses band instruments), this website provides general information about the common instruments and their families. This includes definitions, facts, pictures, etc.


Band has always been a favorite and important thing to me ever since I picked that elective in the sixth grade. I decided to make this website about band because I wanted to share my love of band with people and wanted to inform people about the different instruments and how they work.


Because of Covid, I was in virtual school for one half of sixth grade. When I walked into the band hall for the first time, I was amazed but confused about all the various instruments I saw. I wanted to make this website so that another new band member can pick up their instrument with full knowledge and confidence about it.
