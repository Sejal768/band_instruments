Woodwind

A group of instruments that are or were made of wood and are cylinders or narrow pipes with openings, tone holes, and a mouthpiece. This includes: flutes, oboes, clarinets, saxophones, and bassoons.

Brass

A group of instruments that are generally made of brass, with a U or V shaped mouthpiece, and require the player to buzz, or vibrate, their lips into the mouthpiece. This includes: trumpets, french horns, trombones, baritones, and tubas.

Percussion

A diverse family of instruments that is played by striking, beating, tapping, or shaking the instrument. This includes (but is not limited to): drum sets, maracas, timpanis, bells, snares, etc.
