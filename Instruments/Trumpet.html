Trumpet

About This Instrument

The trumpet is one of the most popular brass instruments for beginning band players. It has a bold, brassy sound which is perfect for fanfares. It is the highest instrument in the brass family and is usually pitched in the B flat key. Trumpets were originally used while hunting in the 1500â€™s and gained popularity in orchestras, jazz bands, and many more bands. Countries all over the world have their own modified version of the trumpet, each having different physical features to accommodate with their different sounding music, but the most common is the B flat trumpet, although A, C, D, E flat, E, low F, and G pitched trumpets also exist. Military bands also use this powerful instrument for many of its marches, songs, and tribute songs.
