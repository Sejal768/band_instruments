Percussion

About This Instrument

Percussion is actually a whole family of instruments ranging from maracas to drum kits to snares. Each instrument is unique and provides a different sound to the score and orchestra or band is playing. Students in bands can be assigned different percussion instruments to play in a piece. Due to the different instruments included, some may have fast, marching parts and others may have slow, mellow ones. Instruments like the snare do rolls and rhythms along with the band while xylophones and glockenspiels usually play slow and mysterious melodies with the woodwinds.
